Everything you need to know about AMAN

AMAN — Your Personal Travel Agent

AMAN is a revolutionary AI-powered travel token built on Solana. It combines the power of blockchain technology with the luxury of personalized travel services. AMAN is not just a token — it's your personal travel agent that remembers your preferences, anticipates your needs, and makes every journey effortless.

📜 Historical Connection

The name AMAN is inspired by the legendary Aman Resorts — the world's most exclusive hotel chain, founded in 1988. Aman means "peace" in Sanskrit, reflecting the brand's philosophy of tranquility and luxury. AMAN token carries this legacy into the digital age, offering holders access to the world's finest travel experiences.

🌟 Vision & Mission

Vision: To become the world's leading digital travel companion, making luxury travel accessible and seamless for everyone.

Mission: To bridge the gap between traditional luxury travel and decentralized technology, offering a single card that handles all travel needs — from booking hotels and flights to arranging private yachts and concierge services.
